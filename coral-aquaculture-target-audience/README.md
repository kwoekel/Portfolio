# Coral Aquaculture Brand — Target Audience Profile

### **1. The Hook *(1 sentence)***
Transformed raw Facebook and Instagram analytics into a full target audience strategy — complete with three buyer personas, geographic expansion opportunities, and content recommendations — for a growing coral aquaculture brand.

### **2. Client / Context *(1–2 sentences)***
A small coral aquaculture business based in the Pacific Northwest, selling reef-safe corals to hobbyists locally and online. The brand was growing rapidly on social media but lacked a structured audience profile to guide marketing decisions.

### **3. The Problem**
The business had real momentum — strong follower growth, six-figure video views, and high viral reach — but no clear picture of *who* was watching, *where* they were located, or *what* would convert them from viewers into customers. Without a defined target audience, content and ad spend decisions were made by instinct rather than data.

### **4. My Role**
Owned the full analysis end-to-end: ingesting and interpreting raw social media data (Facebook Audience Insights, Instagram Demographics, and an Instagram Potential Audience CSV export), synthesizing cross-platform patterns, building the audience personas, and writing the strategic recommendations document.

### **5. The Solution *(your process)***
- Parsed and cross-referenced Facebook and Instagram analytics data — including a UTF-16 encoded CSV export of potential audience demographics — to identify consistent patterns across platforms
- Identified the core audience segment (males aged 35–44, Pacific Northwest-based, reef-keeping hobbyists) and two secondary segments (aspiring aquarists aged 25–34 and international enthusiasts)
- Mapped geographic distribution across current followers vs. potential audience to surface untapped metropolitan markets
- Analyzed content performance data showing short-form video drives 98% of views and 100% of new follows, with 96.7% of views from non-followers — indicating strong viral discovery potential
- Delivered a structured Markdown document with demographic tables, persona profiles, competitive positioning, and actionable growth strategies

### **6. The Result *(lead with numbers)***
- **Primary metric:** Delivered a complete 3-persona target audience profile covering demographics, psychographics, geographic distribution, and content behavior
- **Secondary metrics:** Identified 10 major US metro areas as expansion targets; surfaced the 55+ demographic as an underserved segment representing 36.3% of the potential audience
- **Qualitative impact:** Gave the brand a clear strategic foundation for paid advertising, content planning, and community building — moving from gut-feel to data-driven decision-making
